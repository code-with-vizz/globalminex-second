Dr. Manoj Gaur

Director & Principal Geologist

Dr. Manoj Gaur serves as the Director and Principal Geologist of Global Minex Pvt. Ltd. bringing over 24 years of international experience in mineral exploration, project management, and geospatial technologies. His expertise spans resource modeling, multi-commodity exploration, and the interpretation of diverse mineralization styles. Known for transforming complex geological data into strategic insights, Dr. Gaur has consistently delivered high-impact solutions across continents. Under his visionary leadership, Global Minex has expanded its presence to more than 14 countries, setting new benchmarks in technical excellence and innovation. Key milestones include leading two projects launching Gemfields’ emerald and gold operations in Ethiopia and Mozambique, and serving on the board of WGM in Ethiopia. Dr. Gaur holds a master’s degree in geology and a doctorate from India, along with a master’s in geo-information technology from the University of Glasgow (UK).

Affiliations: Fellow Society of Economic Geology, AusIMM, Geological Survey of India, ISRS
